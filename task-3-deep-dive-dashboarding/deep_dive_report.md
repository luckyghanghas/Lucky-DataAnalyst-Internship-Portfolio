# Task 3 - Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding

## Core KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue | Sum of net order revenue | Shows top-line business scale. |
| Average Order Value | Revenue / Orders | Measures basket strength and upsell opportunity. |
| Gross Margin | Gross Profit / Revenue | Shows whether growth is profitable. |
| Return Rate | Returned Orders / Total Orders | Highlights product-fit and customer satisfaction risk. |
| Customer Rating | Average review score | Captures experience quality after purchase. |

## Deep-Dive Focus
I focused the deep dive on **channel and category performance** because the strongest business decision is where to invest the next marketing and merchandising budget.

## Findings
- Website is the largest revenue channel, but mobile app performance is close enough to justify app-first retention campaigns.
- Audio products bring meaningful revenue but carry higher return risk, so product detail pages and post-purchase support should be improved.
- Regions with above-average returns should be reviewed for delivery delays and product mix differences.
