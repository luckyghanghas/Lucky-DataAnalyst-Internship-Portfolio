# Final Presentation Deck - Retail Sales Analytics

## Slide 1 - Title
Retail Sales Analytics: From Clean Data to Growth Decisions

## Slide 2 - Business Question
Where should the business invest next to grow revenue without damaging margin or customer experience?

## Slide 3 - Dataset and Preparation
Raw sales data was cleaned for duplicate orders, mixed date formats, missing values, inconsistent region names, and revenue outliers.

## Slide 4 - Current Performance
Show KPI cards: revenue, orders, average order value, gross margin, return rate, and average rating.

## Slide 5 - Revenue Mix
Website and Mobile App drive most revenue. Retail Store remains useful but should be treated as a support channel, not the main growth engine.

## Slide 6 - Product and Category Story
Top products create strong revenue, while categories with higher return rates need better product education and customer support.

## Slide 7 - Deep-Dive Findings
Channel and category analysis shows the business should focus on digital retention and return reduction at the same time.

## Slide 8 - Statistical Validation
Use the two-proportion z-test to compare high-satisfaction rates between Website and Mobile App orders.

## Slide 9 - Recommendations
Invest in app retention, improve product detail pages for high-return categories, and create a weekly KPI dashboard for leadership.

## Slide 10 - Next Steps
Automate dashboard refresh, add customer lifetime value, and connect marketing spend data to calculate true campaign ROI.
